CIA Lab 1: Confidentiality - Why It's Important

If we don't encrypt our data and make it unreadable to strangers, it might be seen by the wrong people, or even tampered with by hackers. One way we can encrypt a message in order to ensure its confidentiality is by utilizing the Caesar Cipher.
